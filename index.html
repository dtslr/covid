What can I do to prevent spreading?

   Wash your hands frequently with soap and water. Use paper towels or napkins.

   Keep distance from others. 1,5 metres should be enough.

   Stay safe at home. No parties or events and limit the number of visitors.

   Do not shake hands.

   Cough and sneeze in the inside of your elbow.
